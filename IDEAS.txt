Theme: 10 seconds

IDEAS:
  * Thief sneaks in. Steals treasure. Has 10 seconds to run away.
    - Levels are heavily guarded.
    - Bad guys everywhere.
    - Twitch movement.
    - Thief can't attack, only defend.
    - Genres:
      * A top-down 2D game would work well here.

  * Survive by adding 10 seconds each time.
    - Collect an item, more 10 seconds to live
    - Fail to get those items, dead.
    - Genres:
      * A runner would fit well here.
      * A space shooter would fit well.

  * Solve a puzzle in 10 seconds.
  * Minigame collection.
    - You have 10 seconds to win each minigame
